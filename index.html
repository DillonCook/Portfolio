Who I Am..

Hey there! My name is Dillon, and I'm a Front End Developer.

    I'm a stereotypical Front End Developer who loves coffee. I discovered my love for web development back in 2018. I passed the Treehouse Front End Development course mid-2019, but that's not all. There were numerous other courses I was working through and side-projects I was cultivating.

Since then, I have created several websites for local businesses and friends who need an online presence. Not a CMS presence, but a powerful, customized presence.

Like most developers, I spend too much time after work and on weekends learning new technologies or new tricks and techniques for existing ones. Being a web developer is exciting, challenging, and fulfilling which makes learning something I look forward to every day.
